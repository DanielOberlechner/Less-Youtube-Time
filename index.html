"Less Youtube Time" - Chrome Plugin
This plugin tries to help you save your precious life time. If you open Youtube its very easy to get lost and use hours and hours of your personal time to watch stupid videos. My plugin tries to help you to spend less Time on Youtube. What is does is really simply. It disables all Video recommandations so you will see less 'intresting' videos and therefore spend less Time on Youtube.

That is how the Plugin 'Less Youtube Time' was born.
Download 'Less Youtube Time' Plugin

Functions

Never again click off the Youtube Autoplay Button
Youtube does not remeber if you have Autoplay on or off. Therefore they activate as standard this button and so after every session you have to click it manually off.
Youtubte's Sidebar recommendations
Youtube remembers videos which you liked to watch in the past. So they try to recommend you videos which you probably would like to watch.
Youtube's Endscreen Videos
After you've watched a whole Youtube Video a 4x3 Grid opens up new videos. They show you a few different categories so that the chance you click one of those videos increases.
Youtube's Merchandise Banner
There are on some channel Merchandise Banner and thos are now gone too. Feel free now.
