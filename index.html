"Less Youtube Time" - Chrome Plugin
This plugin tries to help you save your precious life time. If you open youtube its very easy to get lost and use hours and hours of your personal time to watch stupid videos on Youtube. My plugin tries to help you to spend less Time on Youtube. What is does is really simply. It disables all Video recommandations so you will see less 'intresting' videos and therefore spend less Time on Youtube.
So the Plugin name 'Less Youtube Time' was born.
Download 'Less Youtube Time' Plugin
Never again click off the Youtube Autoplay Button
Youtube does not remeber if you have Autoplay on or off. Therefore they activate as standard this button and so after every session you have to click it manually off.
Youtubte's Sidebar recommendations
Youtube remembers videos which you liked to watch in the past. So they try to recommend you videos which you probably would like to watch.
Youtube's Endscreen Videos
After you've watched a whole Youtube Video a 3x3 Grid opens up new videos. They show you a few different categories so that the chance you click one of those videos increases.
Never see annoying Youtube recommandations again
You can use your time for sure for better things as spending your time on Youtube. Use your free time to learn something new in life or spend it with you family or whatever your thing is. Youtube takes your time but after a day you will forget what you have seen so there is nothing good.
